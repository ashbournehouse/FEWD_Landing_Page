Social Media Landings

As this is a demo blog, being published on a unprotected GitHub pages site, I don't want the social media links to lead anyone, or any scraping bot, to genuine social media pages; instead they land here.

Having made the page, it's a good excuse to experiment with html 'cards' although my research shows these simply to be reusable classes that look a bit like business cards, or anything else from a conventional, physical card index. A .card css class does seem to be included in many of the popular css frameworks like 'Tailwind', 'Bootstrap' and 'Materialize'.
